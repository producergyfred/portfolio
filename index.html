ABOUT

Gyfred Nyagwang’ Onyango, widely known as Producer Gyfred is a Kenyan ICT trainer, web developer, graphic designer based in Nairobi, Kenya.

He currently serves as an ICT Trainer at Mathare Peace Initiative (MPI Kenya), where he work with youths from underserved communities to help them develop practical digital skills for employment and entrepreneurship. He specializes in web development, digital design, Physical & online ICT training sessions, with a strong focus on website creation, visual communication, and digital innovation.

Besides his training work, he collaborate with individuals and organizations on website development, brand identity design, and Search Engine Optimization. Through both professional and community-based programs, he promotes the use of technology as a tool for social and economic empowerment.
